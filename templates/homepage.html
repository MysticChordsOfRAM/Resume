The Digital Resume and Portfolio

Operating at the intersection of economics and data engineering, my approach to analysis is defined by total ownership of the data lifecycle. Rather than viewing forecasting as a purely statistical exercise, I treat it as a systems engineering challenge: building the infrastructure to ingest raw data, architecting the SQL pipelines that control it, and developing the reproducible models that drive decision-making. Whether projecting complex state revenue streams or simulating labor market dynamics, I prioritize transparency and scalability, constantly expanding my technical toolkit to modernize how economic insights are generated and delivered.


Regional Economic Consulting

I first picked up a project with REC in 2024, when they wanted to use data from IPEDS to model the labor capacity of university students. I have been on at least one project with them continuously since then.

Florida Department of Revenue

I was hired by the Department in 2021, and have been happily employed there since. Currently, I hold the title Senior Research Economist, having quickly worked my way from the entry level position I started in. My duties at the department include:

  • Forecasting Tax Revenue
  • Scoring Pending Legislation
    • and
  • Producing Webforms and Public Data
As part of my duties, I have successfully automated every web form we produce, as well as investigated and validated the data down to source.

Peace Corps Ghana

From June 2018 to March of 2020, I was an Education Volunteer in Peace Corps Ghana. I taught Math and Computers at Adina AME Zion Junior High School to approximately 300 students. In my time there, I started an extracurricular club for students that wanted more class time, funded and painted a world map mural, and worked with the community to start a poultry farm that would provide the school with additional funding. Unfortunately, my time in the country was cut short by the Pandemic, but I was there long enough to gain a lasting respect for other cultures, and an understanding of all the challenges in developing sustainable change.
